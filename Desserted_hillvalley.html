TO THE DECADES OF THE 1970s

The decade is a reminisince of how things were and how things could have been. The decade initially took of
with no drastic change in people's life. But as the decade started to take shape, we saw the ancestors of
today's things taking shape. The first Apple , A bye bye to the board-games, hello retro sunglasses are just
a whisker of what happened. The 1970s is a decade where the world as we see it today was a caterpillar
and had just started to bloom as a butterfly.
